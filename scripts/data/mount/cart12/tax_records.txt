TAX FILING GUIDANCE

/////////////////////////////


Per Imperial Tax Code &14.2_c,

transit tolls paid during

commercial operations are

fully deductible from annual

merchant levies provided that

the vessel operator maintains

proper documentation as

specified in Treasury

Regulation &14.2_c_(1)-(4) and

submits Form IMP-1040 before

the end of fiscal year.

Deductions claimed without

supporting documentation are

subject to disallowance under

&14.7(a) and may result in

assessment of penalties per

Imperial Decree 447. Retain

this receipt for no less than

seven (7) solar cycles from

the date of filing. For

questions regarding

documentation requirements,

contact the Bureau of

Commercial Transit at Regional

Office 7, Sector 12

Administrative Complex, or

consult Publication 609

(Transit Toll Deduction

Guidelines for Merchant

Vessels).

NOTICE: Failure to maintain 

proper documentation may

result in audit proceedings

per Imperial Decree 447.
